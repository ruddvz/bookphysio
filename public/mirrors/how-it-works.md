# How to Book a Physiotherapist Online in India

> Source: https://bookphysio.in/how-it-works
> Last updated: 2026-04-11

Book a physio session in 4 clear steps with no calls, no waiting, and no guesswork about who to trust.

---

## For Patients — 4 Steps to Your Session

### Step 1: Search
Enter your condition (e.g. back pain, knee injury) or a physiotherapist's name, plus your location.

### Step 2: Choose Provider
Compare expert physiotherapists by specialty, rating, session fees, and distance from you.

### Step 3: Pick a Slot
Select your preferred date and time from the live availability of the provider.

### Step 4: Book Instantly
Confirm your booking with a quick OTP and get expert care. You will receive a confirmation SMS with the details.
