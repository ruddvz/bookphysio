## How It Works

Four steps from search to session. Finding a physiotherapist should not feel like a research project. On BookPhysio, most people go from their first search to a confirmed booking in under a minute.

1. Search — Start with your city or what is bothering you, like back pain or a knee injury.

4. Start your session — Meet your physiotherapist at the clinic or at home, and focus on getting better.

## Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do you verify physiotherapists?
A: We check every physiotherapist's IAP or State Council registration against public registers before they can appear on the platform.

Q: Can I book a home visit?
A: Yes. Providers who offer home visits show their coverage areas and fees on their profile. You can filter search results to show only home-visit providers.

Q: How are prices shown?
A: Each provider card shows session fees in INR. At checkout you see the full breakdown including GST and payment method.

Q: What happens after I book?
A: You get an SMS confirmation with the appointment details. The appointment also appears in your Patient Dashboard where you can view, reschedule, or cancel.

Q: Can I cancel or reschedule?
A: Yes. Cancellations are free up to 4 hours before the session. Inside the 4-hour window, a cancellation fee may apply.
